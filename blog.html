How to Resize Images Without Losing Quality

Resizing images can often reduce quality. Using Imagetool's Resizer, you can maintain sharpness and proportion while changing dimensions. Remember to keep "Maintain Ratio" enabled to avoid distortion.

Tips for Compressing Images for Web Use

Large images can slow down your website. Image Compressor allows you to reduce file size while keeping visual quality high. Test different quality levels to find the perfect balance.

Best Practices for Cropping Photos

Cropping focuses attention on the main subject. Use Crop Tool to trim unnecessary parts. Consider the rule of thirds and maintain aspect ratio for consistent design.

Extracting Text from Images Easily

Scanning documents and screenshots is easy with Image to Text. OCR technology converts images into editable text, saving time on manual typing.

Picking the Right Colors for Your Design

Color Picker lets you identify exact HEX color codes from any image. This ensures color consistency across branding, web design, and graphic projects.

How to Blur Backgrounds Like a Pro

Blurring backgrounds emphasizes subjects. With Blur Background, adjust intensity easily and create professional-looking images for social media, presentations, or portraits.
