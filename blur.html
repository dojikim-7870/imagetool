Create Professional Portraits by Blurring Image Backgrounds

Get That "Portrait Mode" Look on Any Photo

You don't need an expensive DSLR camera to create stunning photos with a beautifully blurred background. This effect, often called "bokeh" or "depth of field," helps your main subject pop and eliminates distracting backgrounds. Our Background Blur tool lets you apply this professional-looking effect to any image, making it perfect for portraits, product photos, or just making your pictures stand out on social media.

When to Use a Blur Effect

  • Emphasize a Subject: Blur the background to draw all the attention to a person, pet, or object in the foreground.
  • Hide a Messy Background: Quickly clean up a photo by blurring out clutter or unwanted details in the background.
  • Protect Privacy: Easily blur out sensitive information, faces, or license plates in an image before sharing it online.

How to Control the Blur Intensity

Using the "Blur Radius" Slider:

Our tool features a simple slider to control the strength of the blur effect.

  • A lower value (e.g., 2-5px) creates a subtle, natural-looking blur.
  • A higher value (e.g., 10-20px) produces a very strong, artistic blur that can completely obscure the background.

Experiment with different values to find the perfect look for your photo.
