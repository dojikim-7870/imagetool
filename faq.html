Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some common questions about using Imagetool's online image tools. If you cannot find an answer here, feel free to contact us via our Contact page.

1. How do I resize my image without losing quality?

Use the Image Resizer. Upload your photo, set the desired width or height, and keep the "Maintain Ratio" option checked to avoid distortion. The tool uses client-side processing for fast results without uploading your image to a server.

2. How can I reduce my image file size?

The Image Compressor allows you to lower the file size of JPG or PNG images. Adjust the quality slider to find the balance between quality and file size. You can preview the compressed image before downloading.

3. How do I crop an image to a specific area?

With the Crop Tool, upload your image and drag the selection box to choose the area to keep. Click "Crop & Download" to save the cropped image. For exact dimensions, you can resize the cropped image afterwards using the Resizer tool.

4. Can I extract text from an image?

The Image to Text (OCR) tool allows you to convert scanned documents or photos containing text into editable text. Upload your image, then click "Run OCR". The recognized text will appear below, ready for copy or download.

5. How do I pick a specific color from an image?

Use the Color Picker. Upload your image and click anywhere on it to get the exact HEX color code. This is useful for web design, graphics, or color matching.

6. How do I blur the background of an image?

The Blur Background tool allows you to add a blur effect to your photo. Upload your image, adjust the blur intensity slider, and download the edited image. Ideal for creating profile pictures or emphasizing subjects.

7. Are my images safe?

Yes. All tools process images on your browser (client-side). Images are not stored on any server, ensuring privacy and security.
